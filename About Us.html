Who We Are

EcoJars Jamaica is an eco-friendly startup based in Kingston, Jamaica. We aim to reduce plastic waste by providing beautiful, reusable glass jars made from recycled materials. We are proud to be part of Jamaica's green future, creating jobs while protecting the environment.
