EcoJars: Beautiful, Sustainable Glassware

EcoJars are reusable glass jars made from 100% recycled materials. They come in various sizes and colors, ideal for storing food, drinks, or crafts. Each jar is handmade by Jamaican artisans and helps reduce plastic waste.
